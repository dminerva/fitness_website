Beginner

This is a 3 day/ week, A/B, linear progression routine, with the main goal of building your strength base. Meaning that there are 2 workouts you will alternate between. For example, week 1: monday;A, wednesday; B, friday; A. Next week: monday; B, wednesday; A, friday; B.

You will increase weight on the main lifts(squat, deadlift, bench press, overhead press and row) by 5 lbs each session, with the exception of the deadlift which you will increase in weight by 10 lbs.

When you fail to complete a set, next time decrease the weight by 10% and continue linear progression. When you fail 3 times you can are ready to move on to the intermediate program. This can take anywhere from 3-6 months.

You need to be getting enough sleep and eating enough food!
